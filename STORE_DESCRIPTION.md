# Meeting Notes — Store Description

**Never lose track of what was said again.** Meeting Notes automatically records, transcribes, and summarizes your meetings using AI that runs entirely on your own PC — no subscriptions, no cloud uploads, no one listening but you.

Just start a recording before your call and let Meeting Notes do the rest. It captures both your microphone and system audio simultaneously, then hands the recording off to Whisper — the same speech-to-text technology trusted by researchers worldwide — to produce a clean, accurate transcript. When you're ready, a local AI model reads the transcript and delivers a concise summary: who said what, what decisions were made, and what action items came out of it. Everything happens on your machine in minutes, with no internet connection required.

Your meetings stay yours. Unlike cloud-based tools that send your conversations to remote servers, Meeting Notes never transmits your audio or transcripts anywhere. All recordings, transcripts, summaries, and notes are stored locally in a single folder you control. Delete the folder and everything is gone — it's that simple. For professionals who handle sensitive conversations, confidential client calls, or internal strategy discussions, that peace of mind is priceless.

Staying organized is effortless. Group meetings into folders by project, client, or team. Annotate any meeting with your own notes alongside the AI summary. Use the built-in chat to ask follow-up questions about a specific meeting or an entire folder of meetings at once. Meeting Notes even watches for apps like Zoom, Microsoft Teams, or Slack and can prompt you to start recording the moment they come into focus — so you never forget to hit record.

Meeting Notes is a one-time download with no ongoing fees. No account required, no usage limits, no data ever leaving your computer. Just fast, private, AI-powered meeting notes — exactly when you need them.
